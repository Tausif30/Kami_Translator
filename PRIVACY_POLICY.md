# Privacy Policy for Kami Translator

**Last Updated:** December 14, 2025

## Data Collection and Usage

Kami Translator is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

### Data We Collect

**Text Content for Translation:**
- When you use Kami Translator, the text you choose to translate is sent to Microsoft Azure Translator API for processing
- This text is transmitted securely via HTTPS
- Microsoft processes the text according to their own privacy policy: https://privacy.microsoft.com/

**Local Storage:**
- Translation preferences (selected target language)
- Per-tab translation state
- All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
- No data is sent to our servers

### Data We DO NOT Collect

- We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- We do not track your browsing history
- We do not collect analytics or usage statistics
- We do not share any data with third parties (except Microsoft Azure for translation)
- We do not use cookies

### Third-Party Services

**Microsoft Azure Translator API:**
- Translations are processed by Microsoft Azure Translator
- Text is sent to Microsoft's servers for translation only
- Microsoft's privacy policy applies: https://privacy.microsoft.com/
- We do not control how Microsoft processes this data

### Data Security

- All communications with Azure Translator API use HTTPS encryption
- Translation preferences are stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers

### Your Rights

- You can clear all stored preferences by removing the extension
- You control what text is sent for translation
- No personal information is collected

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
